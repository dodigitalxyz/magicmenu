Why Choose QR Menus Over Physical Menus?

Cost Efficiency

Physical Menu: Reprinting costs for updates can add up.
Digital Menu: Updates are free with a QR code.

Flexibility and Real-Time Updates

Physical Menu: Updates require redesign and reprinting.
Digital Menu: Change your menu instantly.

Ease of Use

Physical Menu: Susceptible to wear and tear.
Digital Menu: Guests browse easily on their devices.

Hygiene

Physical Menu: Can spread germs between guests.
Digital Menu: No physical contact required.

Sustainability

Physical Menu: Increases paper waste.
Digital Menu: Reduces waste with digital updates.

Interactive Features

Physical Menu: Static content.
Digital Menu: Includes images, videos, and more.

Initial Setup Cost

Physical Menu: Primarily printing costs.
Digital Menu: Initial setup cost but long-term savings.

Instant Access to Menus

With just a QR code, guests can instantly access the latest menu without waiting for staff. No more printed menus, no more delays.

  • No wait time for physical menus
  • Easy access for all guests at the table
  • View the latest menu in real time

Real-Time Menu Updates

Update your menu anytime without reprinting. Our solution ensures your menu reflects the freshest dishes, seasonal offerings, and any changes instantly.

  • Update the menu in real time
  • Save on printing costs
  • Offer promotions and dynamic pricing

Shared Order Basket

All members of the table can scan the QR code and share the same order basket, making group ordering easy and transparent for everyone.

  • Collaborative ordering for families or groups
  • View and track orders in real time
  • Seamless communication between guests

How It Works

Step 1

Place a unique QR code on each table or room.

Step 2

Guests scan and access the up-to-date menu instantly.

Step 3

Guests order, track preparation time, and pay online/offline.
